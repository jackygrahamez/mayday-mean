Text SOS Alert exists as both a webservice and stand alone app. Due to the variations in platforms, Text SOS Alert handles messaging an contact information differently depending on platforms. The android version is a stand alone app, while the iOS version is a webservice.

On Android, personal information which you choose to provide will be retained by Text SOS Alert on your device. Messages transmitted by Text SOS Alert will exist on other services and will be outside the control of Text SOS Alert.

On iOS devices, a web service retains contact information and sends text messages via email over a restful API.

In the future we may choose to move the Android platform to the webservice.

Text SOS Alert makes no guarantees regarding your information security.
