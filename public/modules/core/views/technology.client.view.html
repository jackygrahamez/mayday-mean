Platforms

MayDay is still in Alpha testing phase. It is limited to a small group of testers. There is currently support for Android and iOS.

Android

On the Android platform, MayDay is integrated with Android Wear devices and the Samsung Gear Fit. We’ve tested Mayday with the Samsung Gear Fit™, Motorola Moto 360™ and LG G Watch R™. It has not been tested but it may work with LG G Watch™, Samsung Gear Live™, Asus ZenWatch™ and Sony SmartWatch 3™.

iOS

Apple iOS devices are currently supported for iPod touch®, iPhone®, iPad® and Apple Watch™.
